Hidden Gems of Hyderabad: Off the Beaten Path Adventures

Mrugvani National Park



Specialty : A lush wildlife sanctuary home to various flora and fauna.

Best Time to Visit: October to February.

How to Reach: Located near Chilkur, easily accessible by road.

History: Established in 1994 to conserve the local ecosystem.

Address: Chilkur Balaji Temple Road, Hyderabad.

Why Visit: Explore biodiversity and enjoy nature trails.

Aero Plaza



Specialty: A hub for aviation enthusiasts with static aircraft displays.

Best Time to Visit: Anytime for aviation lovers.

How to Reach: Situated near the old airport, reachable by road.

History: A recreational spot developed around aviation themes.

Address: Opp. Begumpet Airport, Hyderabad.

Why Visit: Experience aircraft up close and learn aviation history.

Sudha Cars Museum



Specialty: Home to unique and artistic handmade cars.

Best Time to Visit: All year round.

How to Reach: Located in Bahadurpura, accessible by road.

History: Founded by K. Sudhakar to showcase creative car designs.

Address: 19-5-28/2/A, Bahadurpura X Roads, Hyderabad.

Why Visit: Witness quirky car creations and artistic innovation.

Aazebo Tolichowki



Specialty: A cozy cafe offering a variety of delectable dishes.

Best Time to Visit: Ideal for brunch or evening gatherings.

How to Reach: In the heart of Tolichowki, easily reachable.

History: A culinary destination known for its welcoming ambiance.

Address: 8-1-400/61, Brindavan Colony, Tolichowki, Hyderabad.

Why Visit: Relish delicious food in a relaxed atmosphere.

Malkam Cheruvu



Specialty: A serene lake surrounded by greenery.

Best Time to Visit: Early morning or late afternoon.

How to Reach: Situated near Shilparamam, accessible by road.

History: An ancient lake with cultural significance.

Address: Near Shilparamam, Madhapur, Hyderabad.

Why Visit: Enjoy tranquility and unwind by the lakeside.
